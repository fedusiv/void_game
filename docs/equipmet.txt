Equipment.

Equipment devide to 4 types:
    Weapon,
    Components,
    Cloth,
    Usable

Each equipment has
	name : name of equipment
	level : level of power of equipment
	desc: lore information about equipmen
	skills: list of skills, that equip use
	info: some information about equip
	params: like armor, or lifetime

Weapon
it is premanent equip for making some actions( damage, heal, magic, etc)
