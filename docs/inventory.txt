Inventory.

Place where player keep all equipment stuff. Inventory has max size, so player can't store everything on his game's way.
In inventory you can equip:
	Weapon
	Head-cloth
	Body-cloth
	Arms-cloth
	Legs-cloth
	Feet-cloth
	Rings, Amulets.

Each equipment has own level, it sign the power of equipment
For some equipment also needs type params. Example, for equip ring1 you need 3 Energy, for equip ring2 you need 2 Energy, but you have 4, so you can equip only one of it, if you will have 4 Energy params point you can equip both.
